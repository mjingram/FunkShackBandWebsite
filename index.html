About

Funk shack is a New Orleans style cover band based out of Denver, Colorado. The four piece consists of Ethan Wackerman (Lead Vocals, Keyboard and Guitar), Michael Ingram (Tenor Sax, Guitar), Connor Szostak (Vocals and Bass), and Garret Corneliussen (Drums). The band started in 2019 when a group of friends got together to jam on some old New Orleans funk tunes. Since then, the group has continued to hone their craft in being a high energy party band playing shows throughout Colorado. Their set lists consist of a mix of funk hits from the 60's, 70's and 80's with a focus on New Orleans style music as well as some modern hits designed to get the crowd up and dancing. If interested in booking contact us at funkshackband@gmail.com.
